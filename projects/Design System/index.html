Design System

Intersection

Intersection is an app created for users who want to utilise their fragmented time on meaningful tasks. From my empaphy interviews, I noticed that people tend to spend more time on their phones during the pandemic. I would like to offer a platform for people who don't wish to spend too much time on social media, Tiktok, or games. The app allows users to post quick tasks(e.g.help with translation) or ask questions, and help pthers with their skills and talents.
